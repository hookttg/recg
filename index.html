What is RECG?

RECG is a free software tool developed to perform ECG analysis. This tool (will) permit to read different formats of ECG, to analyze the ECG signal with different algorithms, the visualization of different kinds of plots and to save the results over different formats.

This tool, is still in a very early stage of development, implements some of the best known QRS detection and delineation algorithms with the intention to share it with the scientific community.
